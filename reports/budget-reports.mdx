Budget reports give you a snapshot of how NDIS funding is being spent across your participants — by participant, by support category, or across your whole caseload.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Reports → Finance">
    Click **Reports** in the sidebar, then **Finance**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Budget Utilisation">
    Choose **Budget Utilisation** from the report type list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your filters">
    - **Plan period** — the plan year or PACE period you want to report on
    - **Participant** — all participants or a specific person
    - **Support category** — all categories or a specific one
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate and download">
    Click **Generate Report**, then download as PDF or CSV.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's in a budget report

For each participant and support category:
- Allocated amount
- Approved (not yet invoiced)
- Invoiced (sent to plan manager or ready for PRODA)
- Submitted (PRODA claims lodged)
- Remaining
- Utilisation percentage
- Plan health status

<Tip>
  The Budget Forecast table (Finance → Budget Forecast) is a faster way to get a real-time utilisation snapshot across all participants. Use the budget report when you need a formal document to file or share.
</Tip>
