CoordHub has two staff roles: **Coordinator** and **Admin**. The organisation **Owner** is a special admin-level role assigned to the account that created the organisation — there can only be one owner.

## Role comparison

| Permission | Coordinator | Admin | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| View own participants and activities | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| View all participants (org-wide) | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Log and submit activities | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Approve activities | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Create and send invoices | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Generate PRODA exports | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Add and exit participants | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Access Reports section | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Manage Settings | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Invite and deactivate staff | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Return approved activities to draft | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |

## Inviting a staff member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Team">
    Click **Settings** in the sidebar, then **Team**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invite Staff Member">
    Click **Invite Staff Member** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter details and assign a role">
    Enter the staff member's email address and select their role (Coordinator or Admin). Click **Send Invitation**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Staff member accepts the invitation">
    They'll receive an email from `no-reply@coordhub.au` with an **Accept Invitation** link. Invitation links expire after 48 hours — if they miss the window, you can resend from the Team page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Assigning caseload

After a coordinator accepts their invitation, assign participants to their caseload:

1. Open a participant profile
2. Go to the **Overview** tab
3. Find the **Assigned Coordinator** field and select the staff member

Participants can only be assigned to one coordinator at a time. Admins can view all participants regardless of assignment.

## Deactivating an account

When a staff member leaves your organisation, deactivate their account rather than deleting it. Deactivation preserves all their historical activity records — if you deleted the account, those records would become unattributed.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Team">
    Find the staff member in the team list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Deactivate">
    Click the **⋮** menu on their row and select **Deactivate Account**. Confirm when prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The staff member can no longer log in. Their name still appears on historical activity records, service agreements, and audit logs — the data is preserved exactly as it was.

<Warning>
  Deactivating an account is reversible — you can reactivate it from the same menu. Deleting an account is permanent and is not available in the UI. Contact CoordHub support if you need to delete an account.
</Warning>

## Resetting a team member's password

Staff manage their own passwords via the login page (**Forgot password?** link). If a coordinator is locked out:

1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Find the staff member
3. Click **⋮ → Send Password Reset**

They'll receive a password reset email at their registered address. The link expires after 1 hour.

<Info>
  MFA can be reset from **Settings → Team → ⋮ → Reset MFA**. Only use this if the staff member has lost access to both their authenticator app and their backup codes — it's a privileged action.
</Info>
