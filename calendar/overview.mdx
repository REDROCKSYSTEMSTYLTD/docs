The Calendar section gives you a visual, time-based view of activities logged in CoordHub. It complements the Activities list view — the same data, displayed differently.

## What's shown on the calendar

Activities appear on the calendar on the date they were delivered. The colour coding and display depends on their status:

| Status | Appearance |
|---|---|
| **Draft** | Shown in muted colour — only visible to the coordinator who created it |
| **Pending** | Shows in pending colour — visible to managers in the approval queue |
| **Approved** | Solid colour — confirmed, billable activity |

Running timers show as a live block on today's date while they're active.

## Calendar navigation

- Use the **week** and **month** toggle to switch between views
- Arrow buttons navigate forward and back
- Click **Today** to return to the current period
- Click on any activity block to open the full activity record

## Filtering the calendar

By default the calendar shows your own activities. If you have a manager role, use the **Coordinator** filter to see another staff member's activities, or switch to **All** to see the full organisation view.

<Tip>
  The month view is useful for spotting gaps in your logged time — an empty week during a busy period might mean activities were forgotten or left in draft.
</Tip>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Common questions">
    **Why don't I see all my activities on the calendar?**
    The calendar shows activities by their delivery date. If an activity was logged with the wrong date, it may appear in a different week or month. Check the activity list (Activities in the sidebar) and confirm the date field on any activity that seems missing.

    **Can I print the calendar view?**
    Use your browser's print function (Cmd+P / Ctrl+P) to print the calendar. The month view prints better than the week view. For a more structured activity list for printing, use Reports → Activities and generate a PDF report for the relevant date range.

    **Can I see a colleague's calendar?**
    If you have a manager role, use the **Coordinator** filter at the top of the calendar to switch to another staff member's view. Standard coordinators can only see their own calendar.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
