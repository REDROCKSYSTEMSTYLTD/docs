You can log activities from the calendar view directly — click on a date block to open the activity form with the date pre-filled.

## Creating an activity from the calendar

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Calendar">
    Click **Calendar** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on a date">
    In week or month view, click on the date (or time slot in week view) when the activity occurred. The activity form opens with that date pre-filled.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the activity form">
    Fill in the participant, support item, duration, and case note as you normally would. See [Logging an activity](/activities/logging-an-activity) for the full guide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save or submit">
    Save as draft or submit for approval.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Calendar vs. Activities list

Both the calendar and the Activities list create the same activity records — it's purely a matter of which view you prefer to work from. Some coordinators find the calendar helpful when reconstructing a busy day's work after the fact.

<Tip>
  In week view, you can drag activity blocks to move them to a different time or day — useful if you logged an activity on the wrong date. Dragging updates the activity's date immediately.
</Tip>
