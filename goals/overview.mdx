Goals in CoordHub reflect the goals stated in a participant's NDIS plan. They give you a framework for measuring progress and provide the evidence base for NDIA progress reports.

## Goals are linked to plans

Each goal you add is linked to a specific NDIS plan. When a plan ends and a new plan begins, you add new goals for the new plan period. Historical goals remain on the participant's record for reference.

## Goal status

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **In Progress** | Goal is actively being worked toward |
| **Achieved** | Goal has been met |
| **Discontinued** | Goal is no longer being pursued (participant changed priority, or plan ended) |

## Goals and NDIA progress reports

When you generate an NDIA progress report for a participant, CoordHub pulls the active goals into the report automatically. Activities tagged to a goal provide the supporting evidence.

<Tip>
  Keep goal names close to the wording in the participant's NDIS plan document. When an NDIA planner or LAC reviews the progress report, seeing familiar language makes the report easier to assess.
</Tip>
