title: "What's New"
description: "Product updates and improvements to CoordHub."
---

Stay up to date with everything new in CoordHub. Updates are added here as features ship.

<Update label="13 June 2026" description="Finance">
  **Batch invoicing is now smarter.** When creating invoices in bulk, CoordHub now automatically filters to NDIA-managed participants and shows the dollar amount for each invoice before you confirm — no more guessing what you're about to send.
</Update>

<Update label="13 June 2026" description="Reports">
  **Register exports are live.** Download PDF and CSV exports of your Risk Register, Supervision Register, Training Register, and CAPA Register directly from the Reports section — audit-ready in one click. Each PDF is formatted to go straight into an evidence pack.
</Update>

<Update label="13 June 2026" description="Finance">
  **Budget forecasting overhaul.** The budget forecast table now shows unsubmitted activities in a separate column so you can see work that's been done but not yet billed. Burn rates are now weighted by participant allocation, all columns are sortable, and you can export the full table to CSV.
</Update>

<Update label="11 June 2026" description="Finance">
  **NDIA payment runs.** CoordHub now tracks NDIA bank EFT payments and matches them to your submitted PRODA claims automatically. View reconciled and unmatched payment runs under Finance → Payments.
</Update>

<Update label="10 June 2026" description="Finance">
  **Write-off workflow.** You can now write off an invoice in CoordHub with a reason recorded in the audit trail — useful for irrecoverable amounts or small rounding differences that aren't worth chasing.
</Update>

<Update label="10 June 2026" description="Finance">
  **NDIS cancellation codes.** When cancelling an activity, CoordHub now captures the correct NDIS cancellation reason — short notice, no show, within 2 business days — and applies the right claim type automatically when you export to PRODA.
</Update>

<Update label="June 2026" description="Finance">
  **myPlace export improvements.** The PRODA CSV export now includes participant names as a column, making it easier to verify your claims before uploading to the NDIA portal. A download button replaces the previous clipboard flow.
</Update>

<Update label="June 2026" description="Finance">
  **Claims history tab.** The Finance section now includes a dedicated Claims tab showing all your submitted NDIS claims, their statuses, and the claim type applied to each line — a clear record of everything that's been lodged.
</Update>
