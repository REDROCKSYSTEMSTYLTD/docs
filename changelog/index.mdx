---
title: "What's New"
description: "Product updates and improvements to CoordHub."
---

Stay up to date with everything new in CoordHub. Updates are added here as features ship.

<Update label="June 2026" description="Compliance">
  **Document control library.** Compliance documents now live in a structured library with versioning, publish/draft status, and acknowledgement tracking. Coordinators see which policies apply to them and can confirm they've read them — creating a signed acknowledgement record automatically.
</Update>

<Update label="June 2026" description="Compliance">
  **Evidence pack builder.** Generate a formatted audit evidence pack from the Compliance section — pull together your four compliance registers (Risk, Supervision, Training, CAPA), incident summaries, and worker screening records into a single downloadable PDF ready for an NDIS auditor.
</Update>

<Update label="May 2026" description="Compliance">
  **Regulatory audit tracking.** Log your NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission audits in CoordHub — including audit type, scope, outcome, and any non-conformances raised. Non-conformances automatically generate a linked CAPA. Upcoming audits surface on the compliance hub.
</Update>

<Update label="May 2026" description="Compliance">
  **Four new compliance registers.** WHS incidents, emergency drills, record disposal, and data breach records now each have a dedicated register — with history tracking, evidence attachments, and bulk CSV import. All four are exportable for audit evidence packs.
</Update>

<Update label="May 2026" description="Compliance">
  **CAPA improvements.** CAPAs raised from incidents or complaints automatically pre-fill with context from the source record. Closing a CAPA now requires an effectiveness review — you document whether the corrective action actually fixed the root cause before it can be marked closed.
</Update>

<Update label="May 2026" description="Compliance">
  **OneDrive sync.** Documents uploaded to CoordHub can now be synced bidirectionally with your Microsoft 365 OneDrive — keeping your coordination files accessible both in CoordHub and in your organisation's document management setup.
</Update>

<Update label="April 2026" description="Compliance">
  **Bulk import for incidents and complaints.** Import historical incident and complaint records via CSV — with a dry-run preview that validates participant NDIS numbers and flags any rows that won't import before you commit. Useful when migrating from another system.
</Update>

<Update label="April 2026" description="Compliance">
  **Corporate risk register.** The Risk register now supports a full supersession model — when you update a risk entry, the previous version is preserved in history rather than overwritten. Owner assignment, review dates, and treatment controls are tracked per version.
</Update>

<Update label="April 2026" description="Compliance">
  **Register review reminders.** CoordHub now sends automated email reminders when a compliance register entry is due for review — including risks, supervision records, and provider compliance documents. Reminders are sent to the assigned owner 30 days before the review date.
</Update>

<Update label="April 2026" description="Participants">
  **Service agreement signing reminders.** CoordHub now sends automated email reminders to coordinators when a service agreement has been sent but not returned within 14 days. Unsigned SAs also surface on the dashboard Expiring Items card.
</Update>

<Update label="April 2026" description="Participants">
  **Scope of Authority register.** Record formal delegations and sign-off authorities for your organisation — who can approve activities, sign service agreements, and authorise payments — with version history and effective date tracking.
</Update>

<Update label="April 2026" description="Reports">
  **Reports Centre.** A new dedicated Reports section replaces scattered export buttons across the app. Activity reports, budget reports, register exports, and data exports are all in one place — with consistent filtering by date range, coordinator, and participant.
</Update>

<Update label="March 2026" description="Compliance">
  **Complaints and feedback improvements.** Complaints now have reference numbers (CMP-XXXX), an investigation officer field, and a severity classification. The complaints list is filterable by severity and outcome. Bulk CSV export added for complaints history.
</Update>

<Update label="February 2026" description="Activities">
  **Calendar view.** Activities are now visible on a week and month calendar — same data as the activities list, displayed across time. Useful for spotting gaps in logged time and reviewing a participant's service history at a glance.
</Update>

<Update label="January 2026" description="Platform">
  **CoordHub launched.** Support Coordination case management, NDIS billing, compliance registers, and participant records — built specifically for Australian NDIS Support Coordination providers.
</Update>

<Update label="13 June 2026" description="Finance">
  **Batch invoicing is now smarter.** When creating invoices in bulk, CoordHub now automatically filters to NDIA-managed participants and shows the dollar amount for each invoice before you confirm — no more guessing what you're about to send.
</Update>

<Update label="13 June 2026" description="Reports">
  **Register exports are live.** Download PDF and CSV exports of your Risk Register, Supervision Register, Training Register, and CAPA Register directly from the Reports section — audit-ready in one click. Each PDF is formatted to go straight into an evidence pack.
</Update>

<Update label="13 June 2026" description="Finance">
  **Budget forecasting overhaul.** The budget forecast table now shows unsubmitted activities in a separate column so you can see work that's been done but not yet billed. Burn rates are now weighted by participant allocation, all columns are sortable, and you can export the full table to CSV.
</Update>

<Update label="11 June 2026" description="Finance">
  **NDIA payment runs.** CoordHub now tracks NDIA bank EFT payments and matches them to your submitted PRODA claims automatically. View reconciled and unmatched payment runs under Finance → Payments.
</Update>

<Update label="10 June 2026" description="Finance">
  **Write-off workflow.** You can now write off an invoice in CoordHub with a reason recorded in the audit trail — useful for irrecoverable amounts or small rounding differences that aren't worth chasing.
</Update>

<Update label="10 June 2026" description="Finance">
  **NDIS cancellation codes.** When cancelling an activity, CoordHub now captures the correct NDIS cancellation reason — short notice, no show, within 2 business days — and applies the right claim type automatically when you export to PRODA.
</Update>

<Update label="June 2026" description="Finance">
  **myPlace export improvements.** The PRODA CSV export now includes participant names as a column, making it easier to verify your claims before uploading to the NDIA portal. A download button replaces the previous clipboard flow.
</Update>

<Update label="June 2026" description="Finance">
  **Claims history tab.** The Finance section now includes a dedicated Claims tab showing all your submitted NDIS claims, their statuses, and the claim type applied to each line — a clear record of everything that's been lodged.
</Update>
