## NDIS support categories

CoordHub uses the standard NDIS support category structure. Each budget you add to a plan is assigned to one category. The category determines which NDIS line items (support items) can be billed against it.

The categories most commonly used in support coordination are:

| Category | Number | Common use |
|---|---|---|
| Daily Activities | 01 | Core supports — personal care, community access |
| Social & Community Participation | 04 | Recreation, social activities |
| Support Coordination | 07 | Support coordination and specialist SC |
| Improved Living Arrangements | 08 | SIL-related coordination |
| Increased Social & Community Participation | 09 | Skills development, education support |
| Finding & Keeping a Job | 10 | Employment-related support |
| Improved Health & Wellbeing | 11 | Allied health supports |
| Improved Learning | 12 | Education-related supports |
| Improved Life Choices | 13 | Plan management |
| Improved Daily Living | 15 | Therapy supports |

Your participants will typically only have budgets in the categories their plan funds — CoordHub only shows relevant categories when logging activities.

## Management types

Each budget can have its own management type, independent of the plan-level setting:

| Management type | How it works in CoordHub |
|---|---|
| **NDIA-managed** | Claims are exported via PRODA and submitted directly to the NDIA. CoordHub generates the PRODA CSV for you. |
| **Plan-managed** | Invoices are sent to the plan manager (a third-party financial intermediary). The plan manager claims from the NDIA on the participant's behalf. |
| **Self-managed** | The participant receives the funds and pays providers directly. Rare for support coordination budgets. |

The budget-level management type takes precedence over the plan-level setting. This is important when a participant has some NDIA-managed budgets and some plan-managed budgets within the same plan — a common scenario.

<Info>
  When you create an invoice, CoordHub routes it correctly based on the management type of the budget the activity drew from. NDIA-managed activities go into the PRODA export; plan-managed activities go into a regular invoice to the plan manager.
</Info>
