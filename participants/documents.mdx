The Documents tab on a participant's profile is where you store files related to that participant — plans, assessments, signed agreements, correspondence, and anything else you need to keep on record.

## Uploading a document

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the participant's Documents tab">
    Open the participant profile and click **Documents** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Upload">
    Click the **Upload** button in the top-right corner. A file picker opens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your file">
    Choose the file from your computer. CoordHub accepts PDF, Word documents (.docx), images (JPG, PNG), and most common file formats. Maximum file size is 50 MB per upload.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a description (optional)">
    Give the file a descriptive name if the filename isn't self-explanatory. This makes it easier to find later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Upload** to confirm. The file appears in the documents list immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's stored here

Documents uploaded to a participant's profile are scoped to that participant — only people with access to that participant's record can see their documents.

Common files stored here include:

- NDIS plans (current and historical)
- Intake assessment documents
- Clinical reports and assessments
- Signed service agreements
- Correspondence with the NDIA, plan managers, or providers
- OPA WA guardian appointment paperwork
- NDIS Consent Form

## Access and visibility

Documents follow the same role-based access as the rest of the participant record. If a team member can view the participant, they can view the documents. Only administrators and above can delete a document.

<Warning>
  Deleted documents cannot be recovered. If you need to remove a document, confirm with your administrator before proceeding.
</Warning>
