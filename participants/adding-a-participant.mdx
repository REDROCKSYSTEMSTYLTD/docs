title: "Adding a participant"
description: "How to create a new participant record in CoordHub — directly or by converting a referral."
icon: "user-plus"
---

There are two ways to add a participant: converting a referral that's already in CoordHub, or adding them directly. Converting a referral is faster because the details you've already captured flow through automatically.

## Option 1 — Convert a referral (recommended)

If you've already received a referral for this person:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Referrals">
    In the sidebar, click **Referrals**. Find the referral record for this person.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the referral">
    Click the referral to open it. Review the details to confirm everything is correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Convert to participant">
    Click **Convert to Participant** in the top-right corner. CoordHub maps the referral data to the participant record — name, NDIS number, DOB, address, clinical flags, and contacts all carry across.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete any missing fields">
    Review the newly created participant record and fill in anything that wasn't in the referral. The intake assessment (under the Intake tab) can be completed separately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Option 2 — Add directly

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the new participant form">
    In the sidebar, click **Participants**, then click **Add Participant** in the top-right corner. Or use Quick Actions → Add Participant.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the required fields">
    Fill in at minimum:
    - **First and last name**
    - **NDIS number** (9 digits)
    - **Date of birth**
    - **Address** (suburb, state, postcode)
    - **Plan management type** (NDIA-managed, Plan-managed, or Self-managed)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a plan manager (if plan-managed)">
    If the participant is plan-managed, link their plan manager from the dropdown. If the plan manager isn't in your directory yet, you can add them under Finance → Plan Managers first, then come back.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the record">
    Click **Save**. The participant is created with a status of **Active**. You can update their status and complete the intake assessment afterwards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next

Once the participant is added, complete their intake assessment under the **Intake** tab, then add their NDIS plan under **Plans**. CoordHub won't be able to track budget or create invoices until a plan with budget categories is set up.

<Warning>
  CoordHub uses the NDIS number to link records across modules. Double-check it against the participant's current plan before saving — correcting it later requires an administrator.
</Warning>
