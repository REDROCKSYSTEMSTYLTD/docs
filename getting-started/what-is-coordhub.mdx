CoordHub is practice management software designed specifically for NDIS Support Coordinators. It replaces spreadsheets, shared drives, and disconnected tools with a single platform that covers everything from participant onboarding through to invoicing and compliance reporting.

## What CoordHub covers

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Participants" icon="users">
    Manage participant profiles, documents, goals, contacts, and the full history of their support journey.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Activities" icon="list-check">
    Log billable and non-billable support coordination activities with built-in timers and an approval workflow.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Plans & Budgets" icon="file-invoice-dollar">
    Track NDIS plan funding by support category, monitor budget utilisation, and forecast spend against remaining funds.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Service Agreements" icon="file-signature">
    Create service agreements from participant intake data, send for signing, and manage the full lifecycle.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Finance & Billing" icon="building-columns">
    Generate invoices, submit NDIS claims, export PRODA files for the myPlace portal, and reconcile payments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reports" icon="chart-bar">
    Download activity, budget, and compliance reports in PDF or CSV — audit-ready in one click.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Who uses CoordHub

CoordHub is designed for Support Coordinators and Specialist Support Coordinators working in Australian NDIS registered organisations. Your access level depends on your role — coordinators manage their own caseload, while administrators and owners have visibility across the full organisation.

## How CoordHub fits into your work

CoordHub connects your participant records to your billing so there's no manual data re-entry. An activity you log for a participant flows directly into invoice creation, NDIS claims, and budget tracking — all linked to the correct support category and funding period automatically.
