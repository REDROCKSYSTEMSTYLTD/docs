CoordHub is practice management software designed specifically for NDIS Support Coordinators. It replaces spreadsheets, shared drives, and disconnected tools with a single platform that covers everything from participant onboarding through to invoicing and compliance reporting.

In a typical coordination practice, information about each participant is scattered across email threads, shared drives, and separate billing systems. Every month-end involves manually reconciling activities, looking up support item codes, and re-entering data into a billing system. CoordHub connects all of these workflows — a case note you write when logging an activity becomes part of the audit trail, the billing record, and the NDIA progress report, all at once.

## Who uses CoordHub

CoordHub is designed for Support Coordinators and Specialist Support Coordinators working in Australian NDIS registered organisations. Your access level depends on your role:

- **Coordinators** — manage their own assigned caseload, log activities, write case notes, and work with participants day to day
- **Administrators** — have organisation-wide visibility, approve activities, manage invoicing and billing, and control settings
- **Owners** — the same as Admin plus organisation-level settings that can't be changed by administrators

If you're a sole coordinator and the owner of your organisation, you'll have access to everything.

## How CoordHub fits into your work

CoordHub connects your participant records to your billing so there's no manual data re-entry. An activity you log for a participant flows directly into invoice creation, NDIS claims, and budget tracking — all linked to the correct support category and funding period automatically.

For example: you log a 1.5-hour Support Coordination session for a participant on a Monday. By the end of the month, that activity is:
- Part of the participant's activity history with your case note attached
- Counted in their Category 07 budget utilisation
- Available for the month-end batch invoice
- Included in the PRODA export when you submit claims to the NDIA
- Reflected in any NDIA progress report you generate

  <Accordion title="Common questions">
    **Is CoordHub only for Support Coordinators?**
    Yes — CoordHub is purpose-built for NDIS Support Coordination and Specialist Support Coordination. It's not designed for SIL providers, support workers, or allied health practitioners.

    **Can I use CoordHub for multiple participants at once?**
    Yes — CoordHub is designed for coordinators managing a full caseload of 15–50+ participants. The dashboard, budget forecast, and reports are all designed to give you visibility across your whole caseload, not just one person at a time.

    **Does CoordHub work on mobile?**
    Yes — CoordHub is a browser-based web app that works on mobile browsers. There's no dedicated app to download. The sidebar collapses automatically on smaller screens.
