CoordHub uses a collapsible sidebar for primary navigation, a header for global controls, and a keyboard shortcut for fast search.

## The sidebar

The sidebar runs down the left side of the screen. It's organised into collapsible groups — click any group heading to expand or collapse it. CoordHub remembers which groups you've opened between sessions.

The main sections are:

| Section | What's here |
|---|---|
| **Participants** | Your participant list and individual profiles |
| **Activities** | Log activities, view pending approvals, running timers |
| **Finance** | Invoices, claims, PRODA export, plan managers, payments |
| **Reports** | Activity, budget, compliance, and staff reports |
| **Calendar** | Activity calendar view |
| **Team** | Staff directory, caseload matrix |
| **Compliance** | Incidents, audits, risks, WH&S, COI register |
| **Messages** | Internal messaging between team members |

## Quick search

Press **Cmd+K** (Mac) or **Ctrl+K** (Windows/Linux) from anywhere in CoordHub to open the search bar. You can search for participants by name or NDIS number, jump to any page in the app, or find a specific activity or invoice.

## The header

The header sits at the top of every page and contains:

- **CoordHub logo** — click to return to the dashboard
- **Quick Actions** — common tasks without navigating first
- **Alerts bell** — compliance alerts and notifications
- **Help** — opens the Help Centre drawer (see [Getting help](/getting-started/getting-help))
- **Profile menu** — account settings, sign out

## On mobile

CoordHub works on mobile browsers. The sidebar collapses automatically on smaller screens — tap the menu icon in the top-left corner to open it.

<Tip>
  Most coordinators keep Participants and Activities expanded in the sidebar and collapse the rest. The collapsed groups still appear when you navigate into them directly.
</Tip>

  <Accordion title="Common questions">
    **How do I switch between organisations?**
    If your account is part of multiple organisations (e.g. you consult across two registered providers), a switcher appears in the top-left corner of the sidebar below the CoordHub logo. Click it to switch organisations. Each organisation is completely separate — participant records, activities, and billing don't mix between organisations.

    **The sidebar is missing some sections — is something wrong?**
    CoordHub shows sidebar sections based on your role. Coordinators don't see the full Compliance section, the Team directory, or Settings. If you need access to a section that's not visible, contact your administrator to discuss a role change.

    **Can I use keyboard shortcuts in CoordHub?**
    Yes. **Cmd+K** / **Ctrl+K** opens quick search. Most standard browser shortcuts (Cmd+R to refresh, Tab to move between form fields, Esc to close modals) work as expected. There are no additional custom keyboard shortcuts at this stage.
