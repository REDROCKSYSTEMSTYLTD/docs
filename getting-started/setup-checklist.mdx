title: "Setup checklist"
description: "A step-by-step checklist to get your organisation fully set up in CoordHub."

---

Use this checklist when you're getting started with CoordHub. Work through it in order — each step builds on the previous one.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in and complete MFA setup">
    Open [app.coordhub.au](https://app.coordhub.au) and log in with the credentials from your invitation email. CoordHub requires multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all accounts — you'll be prompted to set this up on your first login.

    See [First login & MFA](/getting-started/first-login-and-mfa) for step-by-step instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your organisation details">
    Go to **Settings → Organisation** and fill in your organisation's details:
    - Organisation name (as registered with the NDIA)
    - ABN
    - NDIS registration number
    - Primary contact name and email
    - Business address

    Your NDIS registration number is required before you can generate PRODA exports for NDIA-managed participants.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure invoice settings">
    Still in **Settings → Organisation**, scroll to the **Invoice Settings** section:
    - Set your invoice number prefix (e.g. `INV-` or your organisation abbreviation)
    - Set the default payment terms (e.g. 14 days, 30 days)

    These settings apply to all invoices you create in CoordHub.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your team members">
    Go to **Settings → Team** and invite your staff. For each team member:
    - Enter their email address
    - Assign their role (Admin or Coordinator)
    - Click **Send Invitation**

    They'll receive an invitation email and set up their own password and MFA.

    See [Managing users](/settings/managing-users) for a full guide to roles and permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first participant">
    Go to **Participants** in the sidebar and click **Add Participant**. You'll need:
    - The participant's full legal name
    - Their 9-digit NDIS number
    - Date of birth
    - Address
    - Plan management type (NDIA-managed, plan-managed, or self-managed)

    See [Adding a participant](/participants/adding-a-participant) for the full walkthrough.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the participant's NDIS plan">
    Open the participant's profile and go to the **Plans** tab. Click **Add Plan** and enter the plan dates and support budgets from the participant's current NDIS plan document.

    CoordHub can't track budget or create invoices until a plan is set up with budget categories.

    See [Adding an NDIS plan](/plans/adding-a-plan) for the full guide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log your first activity">
    Go to **Activities** in the sidebar and click **Log Activity**. Select the participant, enter the date and time, choose the support item, and write a case note.

    Submit the activity for approval when you're done.

    See [Logging an activity](/activities/logging-an-activity) for detailed instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your M365 calendar (optional)">
    If your organisation uses Microsoft 365 / Outlook, you can connect CoordHub to your calendar so activities appear as calendar events.

    An administrator needs to set this up at the organisation level first — see [Connecting your calendar](/calendar/connecting-your-calendar) for the full guide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your dashboard">
    Return to the dashboard (click the CoordHub logo in the top-left corner). You should now see your participant in the active count and your activity in the pending queue.

    See [The dashboard](/getting-started/the-dashboard) for a guide to what each KPI card means.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The full onboarding sequence above takes about 30 minutes for a single coordinator starting from scratch. Once you've done it once, adding additional participants and activities takes only a few minutes each.
</Tip>
