---
title: "Activating your account"
description: "How to accept your CoordHub invitation and set up your password."
icon: "mail-open"
---

Your organisation owner or administrator sends you an invitation by email. You don't create your own account — access is provisioned for you.

## Before you start

Check that the invitation email came from `no-reply@coordhub.au` or your organisation's registered email address. If you can't find it, check your spam folder before asking for a resend.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the invitation email">
    Look for an email with the subject **"You've been invited to CoordHub"**. Open it and click the **Accept Invitation** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your password">
    You'll be taken to a password setup page. Choose a strong password — at least 12 characters. CoordHub doesn't impose complexity rules, but your password manager's suggestion is a good choice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in">
    After setting your password you'll be redirected to the login page. Enter your email address and the password you just created.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete MFA enrolment">
    CoordHub requires multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all accounts. On your first login you'll be prompted to set this up. See [First login & MFA](/getting-started/first-login-and-mfa) for the full walkthrough.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next

Once MFA is set up, you land on your dashboard. Your role and caseload assignments have already been configured by your administrator — you won't need to set those up yourself.

<Warning>
  Invitation links expire after 48 hours. If your link has expired, ask your organisation's owner or administrator to send a new one from Settings → Team.
</Warning>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Common questions">
    **My invitation email hasn't arrived — what do I do?**
    Check your spam or junk folder first. If it's not there, check that your administrator sent the invite to the correct email address. Invitation emails come from `no-reply@coordhub.au`. If your organisation's email gateway blocks external senders, ask your IT team to whitelist that address.

    **My invitation link has expired — what do I do?**
    Invitation links expire after 48 hours. Ask your organisation's owner or administrator to resend the invitation from **Settings → Team**.

    **Can I change my email address after setting up my account?**
    Contact your administrator or owner — they can update your email address from **Settings → Team**. You'll receive a verification email at the new address before the change takes effect.

    **Can I share an account with a colleague?**
    No — each staff member must have their own individual CoordHub account. Shared accounts create compliance risks because activity records and audit logs are attributed by individual user. If a colleague needs access, ask your administrator to invite them with a separate email address.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
