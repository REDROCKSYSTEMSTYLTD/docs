## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Providers in the sidebar">
    Click **Providers** (or **Directory** depending on your sidebar layout) to open the provider list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add Provider">
    Click **Add Provider** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter provider details">
    Fill in:
    - **Organisation name**
    - **ABN** — the provider's Australian Business Number
    - **NDIS registration number** (if they're a registered NDIS provider)
    - **Contact name, phone, and email** — your main point of contact at this organisation
    - **Service categories** — the NDIS support categories this provider delivers
    - **Address and suburb** — used for location-based matching
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The provider appears in the directory and can be linked to participants.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Linking a provider to a participant

Once a provider is in the directory:
1. Open the participant profile and go to the **Providers** tab
2. Click **Link Provider**
3. Search for and select the provider
4. Choose the support category this provider delivers for this participant

<Tip>
  If a provider serves many of your participants, you only need to add them to the directory once. Link them individually to each participant from the Providers tab on the participant's profile.
</Tip>
