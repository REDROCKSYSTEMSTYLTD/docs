The provider directory in CoordHub is where you record the service providers your participants use — the organisations that deliver the actual NDIS supports (therapists, support workers, SIL providers, etc.).

## Providers vs. plan managers

These are two different things in CoordHub:

| | Providers | Plan Managers |
|---|---|---|
| **What they are** | Organisations delivering NDIS supports to your participants | Financial intermediaries who manage the participant's NDIS funds |
| **Where in CoordHub** | Finance → Plan Managers | Providers section |
| **Linked to** | Specific participants | Specific plan budgets |

## Smart Match

CoordHub includes a **Smart Match** feature that helps you find providers for a participant based on their support needs and location. Smart Match searches the provider directory for providers who match the participant's required support categories.

Access Smart Match from a participant's Providers tab — click **Find Providers** to run a match.

## Provider verification status

Each provider record has a verification status based on their compliance documents:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| ✅ Verified | Key compliance documents are uploaded and current |
| ⚠️ Pending | Some documents are missing or approaching expiry |
| ❌ Unverified | No compliance documents on record |

Verification status is advisory — CoordHub doesn't block you from linking an unverified provider to a participant, but it's a useful prompt to follow up on compliance documentation.
